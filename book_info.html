In this deeply personal memoir, Sarah L. Tringhese invites readers on an intimate journey through her darkest days.
Throughout her life, she has endured profound trauma—but over time, learned to transform her pain into purpose. After reaching a breaking point, Sarah made life-altering choices that began to shape the second half of her story.

Every trial she faced, including her young daughter’s brain tumor diagnosis, played a role in shaping the woman she is today. The lessons she learned along the way continue to guide her daily life.

This memoir is more than Sarah’s story—it’s a powerful
testament to how God shows up in our suffering and transforms it into purpose.

A book for parents, survivors, and anyone standing at the edge of their own “second half,” wondering if healing is still possible.

It is. Sarah is living proof.

This is a real life bio about a young women who became a wife, and then a mother and the struggles and challenges that she confronted head on with her family and her faith. The book demonstrates that through faith we gain strength and with strength, our faith grows stronger. Reading Sarah's book gives one a deeper appreciation of the cards that are dealt to parents of young children who are diagnosed with inoperable cancer....the despair...the pain...the anger...the frustrations..and the mental and physical exhaustion. I encourage others, especially parents with children, to read this book.
